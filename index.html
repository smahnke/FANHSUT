Filipino American History Month

Where: Online

When: Every week of October 2021

Join FANHS Utah Chapter as we celebrate this year's Filipino-American History Month with a series of weekly events to get to know Filipinx-American history and activism. We'll kick off the month with an official recognition of October as Filipino American History Month from the state of Utah, and keep the party going with hip hop demonstrations, film screenings, and more!

About Filipino American Historical National Society

Founded in 2019, FANHS-UT seeks to document and promote awareness of Filipino and Filipino American history, issues, and culture; create inclusive spaces for Filipinos and Filipino Americans in Utah, and empower the future leadership of our community.
