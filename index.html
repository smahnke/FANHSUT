Filipinx American National Historical Society

Utah Chapter

Founded in 2019, FANHS-UT seeks to document and promote awareness of Filipino and Filipino American history, issues, and culture; create inclusive spaces for Filipinos and Filipino Americans in Utah, and empower the future leadership of our community.
